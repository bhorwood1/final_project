The third largest Hawaiian island is home to the majority of Hawaii’s diverse population, a fusion of east and west cultures rooted in the values and traditions of the Native Hawaiian people. A short drive from the modern city lies ‘the country,’ with its beckoning two-lane roads and all-natural beaches where sea turtles bask. This vibrant island is set amidst a tropical paradise that allows you to be as active or as relaxed as you desire. You’ll find world class golf, spas, award-winning restaurants and outstanding shopping.
